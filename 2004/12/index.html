Hi Everyone and Merry Christmas

By Denise Woolsey on December 21, 2004 10:21 PM | 3 Comments

Hi Everyone and Merry Christmas

I can't believe that it is that time of year again already. We have been so flat out over the last month that time has just flown.

The new restaurant is going great and beyond our wildest dreams. It is probably twice as busy as we had thought and already we are talking about re-doing the kitchen in the New Year. Our staff have been fantastic and we have a great new manager in there so again we are lucky.

Nora is mobile and oh my God we have to watch out. She is everywhere and so quick. I wonder where she gets all that energy from? How much fun is Christmas going to be? She hasn't a clue of course except that she wants to eat all the wrapping paper under the tree. I will put some photo's on here.

For those of you who haven't had your christmas cards yet I am sorry. I will still try and write them out and send them. Honestly it has been so manic. How do those of you with more than one child do it. Suddenly another day has gone and i'm so knackered that it's time for bed.


I hope you all have a great Christmas. I am cooking turkey and then going to the beach. Everyone thinks I am mad but it won't seem like Christmas without turkey. Maybe after a few years I will get used to seafood.


lots of visitors planned for next year already beginning in January so let me know if and when you are coming

With love

D&D&Nora
