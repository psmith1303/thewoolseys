Hello everyone

By Denise Woolsey on September 3, 2006 11:27 AM | 1 Comment

Yes Peter I have been very lazy with our updates so here we are.

Less than four weeks to go until the new Woolsey arrives. We had a late scan as my doctor thought I was looking a little small so we know it is another little girl (Erin). As it turns out she is bigger than Nora was and from where I look at things I am huge. Anyway not long now. Nora is beside herself with excitement. Maybe when she realises that she is here to stay it will be a little different. I don't know what it is like where you are but everyone seems to be having babies at the moment.

We are all well. Just coming into our spring and we have already had a few days on the beach. Wonderful. Work is getting busier now that winter is over and the school holidays are just around the corner.

Nora has just woke up from her sleep and we are off to a birthday party so I will have to cut this short.

I will let you all know when the big event happens. Wish us luck

D&D&N&E
