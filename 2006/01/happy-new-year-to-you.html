HAPPY NEW YEAR TO YOU

By Denise Woolsey on January 1, 2006 8:31 PM | No Comments

HAPPY NEW YEAR TO YOU ALL

I thought I had better update this as time once again has escaped and raced by.

Hope all of your Christmasses were good and for those of you in the UK hope it warms up soon. The snow even made our news.

Here we are sweltering in the mid 30's and loving it. The pool has come in to it's own and Nora doesn't want to come out once she is in. Her cousin Amy (and Petra and Lee) visited over Christmas and showed her some skills in the pool which can be scary.

We of course are in our busy season and it is the middle of the summer holidays. It doesn't seem as mad as last year which is good. Mind you we didn't get to spend as much time as we wanted to with all our visitors.

Petra, Lee and Amy we here for a couple of weeks, Paul and Barb dropped in for a couple of days on their way to Sydney and Ally and Paul leave tomorrow after being here for a week. I guess we will have to plan another trip to the UK to see you all properly.

Thanks to all of you for the great gifts for Nora. She was completely overwhelmed and didn't hit real present opening frenzy until late in the evening. Her Grandad provided her with her first motorbike which she started off being scared of but she is now well and truly able to wizz around on it. Again scary. Just like her dad.

Hope you are all well. I will attempt to put some photos on here now.

with love

D&D&N
